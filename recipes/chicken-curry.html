Chicken Curry

Home

Description

A simple chicken curry for those, who can't handle too much spice.

Ingredients

  • 600 g Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Paprikas
  • 400ml Coconut Milk
  • Garlic
  • Paprika Powder
  • Curry Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of Curry Paste

Steps

  1. Wash rice & cook it in the rice cooker.
  2. Cut chicken into strips, spice it with salt, pepper and curry powder and fry it in a pan.
  3. Cut onions, garlic and paprikas. Remove chicken from the pan and fry the vegetables in a wok.
  4. Add chicken into the wok with the coconut milk, curry powder and paste. Let it simmer for 20min.
  5. Serve with rice.
