Strawberry Cake

Home

Description

The easiest strawberry cake in the world.

Ingredients

  • Finished Biscuit Cake
  • 1 Package Cake Glaze
  • 500g Strawberries

Steps

  1. Wash and cut strawberries.
  2. Prepare cake glaze by following the instructions on the package.
  3. Put strawberries onto the biscuit and pour the glaze over them.
  4. Wait for the cake to cool down.
  5. Enjoy.
