Spaghetti Bolognese

Home

Description

A simple and yummy dish, which can be done by anyone.

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • Garlic
  • 500 g Ground Beef
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 200 ml Vegetable Broth
  • 1 Tablespoon Tomato Paste
  • 400g Tomatoes (Can)
  • Ketchup
  • 500g Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Mince up the onion and garlic.
  2. Cook ground beef in a pan until it's brown. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Douse it with vegetable broth. Add tomato paste, tomatoes and ketchup. Let it simmer for 40 min.
  4. Cook spaghetti like usual.
  5. Serve spaghetti with sauce.
